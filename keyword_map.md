## 3. SEO Cannibalization Guardrails
1. **Never use generic titles:** Each page header must clearly reflect its specific sub-topic or product category.
2. **Contextual Anchor Text:** When linking internally, use the target Primary Keyword as the anchor text only when pointing to its designated destination page. For example, any link with anchor text `custom 40oz tumbler manufacturer` must point directly to `/custom-40oz-tumbler-manufacturer/` and nowhere else.
3. **Intent Separation:** Informational guides under `/sourcing-guides/` should cover "how-to" and "guides", whereas pages under the root (or product folders) target commercial transactional queries.
