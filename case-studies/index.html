What These Cases Show

Most B2B drinkware projects are won or lost on preparation: an exact product reference, realistic MOQ, sample approval, packaging detail, inspection scope and destination data. HDS structures the discussion around those checkpoints.

Before you contact HDS

Prepare the details that make B2B sourcing faster.

Clear product, logo, packaging and shipping information helps the team reply with a practical path instead of a generic answer.

  • Product photo, target capacity, material preference and sales channel.
  • Order quantity, sample need and destination country.
  • Logo artwork, logo size, logo position and preferred logo method.
  • Packaging request, carton marks, barcode needs or gift set plan.
  • Target timeline and shipping preference such as DDP, DDU, FOB or EXW.
