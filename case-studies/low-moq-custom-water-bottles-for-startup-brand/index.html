Scenario Requirements and Risks

As a newly launched online boutique, the client wanted to test a custom sports bottle line with minimal upfront capital. They required a low MOQ of 300 pcs, but most China plastic manufacturers require a minimum of 2,000 to 3,000 pcs for custom print. They also needed the bottles to be 100% odor-free and durable.

Illustrative Planning Approach

  • Confirm the exact product and customization scope: Single-color screen-printed custom motivational time-markers, custom brand logo, matching custom color silicone carry loop, and individual retail color boxes.
  • Approve the proposed logo method on the selected surface: Silk Screen Printing (Durable food-grade inks)
  • Review the complete packed sample or packaging proof: Individual custom color-printed cardboard boxes with product features, packed 30 pcs per export master carton.
  • Write the production target only after sample approval: 18 days from sample approval
  • Compare the named shipping scope and destination: DDU Air Freight to London

Scenario Outcome and Acceptance Criteria

Acceptance would require the buyer to approve the exact sample or signed specification, artwork, packaging proof, inspection checklist, carton data, named destination and shipping term.
