The Challenge

The client wanted to match a specific viral pastel color palette with a clean, high-definition laser logo. However, standard shipping for bulky 40oz tumblers is extremely expensive, and any shipping damage or delayed delivery would hurt their launch window. Additionally, Amazon has strict barcode and carton requirements.

The Solution

HDS coordinated Pantone color-direction samples, video confirmation and a physical proofing sample. Packaging was reviewed against the buyer's FBA preparation information, including individual barcode placement, carton labels, carton quantity, dimensions and gross weight. Freight comparison was based on the confirmed packing data rather than the product unit price alone.

The Result

The initial 1,200-piece order was prepared for direct delivery to the nominated FBA destination under the agreed DDP scope. The repeat-order discussion used the approved product, color, logo and packing specification as the reference instead of restarting from a generic catalog image.
