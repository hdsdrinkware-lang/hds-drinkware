Scenario Requirements and Risks

The client wanted to match a specific viral pastel color palette with a clean, high-definition laser logo. However, standard shipping for bulky 40oz tumblers is extremely expensive, and any shipping damage or delayed delivery would hurt their launch window. Additionally, Amazon has strict barcode and carton requirements.

Illustrative Planning Approach

  • Confirm the exact product and customization scope: Custom Pantone color matching for gradient spray paint, laser-engraved brand logo, custom cardboard divider packaging, and FBA-compliant barcode labeling.
  • Approve the proposed logo method on the selected surface: High-precision laser engraving (dishwasher-safe, no peeling)
  • Review the complete packed sample or packaging proof: Individually wrapped in biodegradable polybags, placed in standard egg-crate cardboard dividers to maximize shipping density and reduce cost.
  • Write the production target only after sample approval: 28 days from sample approval to bulk shipment ready
  • Compare the named shipping scope and destination: DDP Sea Freight directly to Amazon FBA Warehouse in California

Scenario Outcome and Acceptance Criteria

Acceptance would require the buyer to approve the exact sample or signed specification, artwork, packaging proof, inspection checklist, carton data, named destination and shipping term.
