Q4 Cost and Quote Variables

  • Base product, selected material and lid or accessory configuration.
  • Logo method, artwork size, number of positions and required proof.
  • Standard box, color box, gift box, insert, card, barcode and carton marks.
  • Sample cost, bulk quantity, inspection scope and packing labor.
  • Final carton dimensions, gross weight, destination and agreed Incoterm.

Compare quotations only when these variables use the same assumptions. A low unit price can become a higher landed cost when packaging volume or freight scope differs.

Approval Checklist Before Bulk Production

  • Exact SKU, capacity, dimensions and food-contact material information available for the quoted model.
  • Logo file, method, size, position and color reference.
  • Color, coating, lid, handle, straw and accessories approved.
  • Packaging structure, barcode, carton quantity and carton marks confirmed.
  • Inspection scope and acceptable result agreed in writing.
