Introduction

Brazil represents one of the most promising but complex markets for custom drinkware. With the 2026 Brazilian Tax Reform now in its implementation phase, B2B importers must account for updated IPI (Industrialized Products Tax) and social contribution fees to remain profitable.

Key 2026 Strategic Advantages for Brazil

HDS Drinkware (Huandingsheng) has optimized its production and logistics flow specifically for the requirements of Sao Paulo and Rio de Janeiro ports:

  • High-Volume Capacity: With a monthly capacity of 22 x 40'HQ containers, we handle orders from 2,000 to 100,000 units with consistent lead times.
  • Tax-Efficient Packing: We optimize pallet loading to reduce "dead space" in containers, effectively lowering the per-unit IPI tax burden through shipping volume efficiency.
  • Compliance Certification: Full Sus304 and BPA-free lab reports ready for Brazilian health and customs inspection.
