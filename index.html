What is the MOQ for custom drinkware orders?

MOQ starts from 200 pcs for many custom drinkware projects. Final MOQ depends on product type, color, logo method, packaging and order season.

Can I customize tumblers with my logo?

Yes. HDS supports logo customization on tumblers, bottles, coffee cups and gift sets. You can send a logo file or product photo for sample and quote discussion.

What logo methods are available for drinkware?

Common methods include laser engraving, silk screen printing, UV printing, heat transfer, labels and packaging logo placement.

Can you provide custom packaging?

Yes. Standard packing is available, and custom color boxes, gift boxes, sleeves, labels, inserts and bundled gift-set packaging can be arranged by project.

Can you ship to Amazon FBA or overseas warehouses?

Yes. Orders ship from China and can be coordinated for Amazon FBA, overseas warehouses or buyer warehouses with DDP or DDU shipping where suitable.

How long does sampling and mass production take?

Stock samples can often ship quickly. Custom or new samples usually take 7-10 days, and mass production is usually 30-35 days depending on product, quantity and packaging.

What types of buyers does HDS Drinkware support?

HDS supports Amazon sellers, TikTok Shop sellers, Shopify brands, gift companies, wholesalers, distributors and corporate gift buyers.

Can you help source multiple products together?

Yes. We offer one-stop procurement support for drinkware, gift bundles, packaging, inspection, and shipment coordination.
