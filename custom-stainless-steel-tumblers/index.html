What Does “FDA or LFGB Compliant” Mean for a Tumbler Quote?

It should mean the supplier can identify the exact product, food-contact components, intended use, destination market and supporting document or test scope. A generic category claim is not enough. Buyers should match every report or declaration to the quoted SKU, material, supplier or manufacturer, date and conditions of use.
